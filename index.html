University Place: Exploring The Luxurious West Side.

Discovering the dynamic University Place. A site of growth and luxury.

University Place was a really interesting place to look at. As I walked around it was astonishing to see the new and modern residential buildings that strecthed all the way from the nearest light rail station to West Campus Village. To me, these building were a strong reminder that the only thing that is constant is change, and it is important to adapt.

Moreover, as a City Planner, University Place made wonder about the structures, malls and people that would come about in the new vicinity and the overall impact they will have on Jersey City and how its perception. As noted, Jersey City is not known as being as influential or vibrant as NYC, but as the population continues to raise, and prices also rise, I infer that Jersey City will become a very important piece in the economic puzzle.
